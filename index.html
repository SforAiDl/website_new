Society for Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning (SAiDL)

We are a student-run, non-profit, professional group of motivated individuals based out of BITS Pilani, working on research and application of Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning. Through involvement in projects with the agenies below, we aim to:
  • Inculcate the spirit of research in Indian universities
  • Form an extensive alumni network of researchers in AI and deep learning, and
  • Collaborate with industry and research labs on cutting-edge research
